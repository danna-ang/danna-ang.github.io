PAG-IBIG MP2 fund projections

Python | ARIMA | Exploratory Data Analysis | Excel

Wondering whether or not worth it to invest in the Modified PAG-IBIG 2 (MP2) Savings Fund 2 (MP2), I tried my hand at some exploratory data analysis on naive projections of the growth of the MP2 dividend rate for the next five years. I then incorporated these predictions into an Excel calculator for computing MP2 returns.



Varied MP2 divident rate predictions are seen for different fitting and extrapolation methods.

ARIMA forecasts show an increasing trend that may temporarily plateau after several years.
